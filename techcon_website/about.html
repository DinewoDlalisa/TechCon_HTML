History

TechCon was established in the early 20th centuries by professional association with the aim to share knowledge and advancement in Tech. The conference became in vogue due to the rise of personal computer and internet.One of the challenges that were faced along the year was defining a specific focus and scope for the conference and the issues of sponsorship as the event was novel in those time. Despite these struugles TechCon still had the passion for the industry and how it could positively impact the community. Fast foward to now,Techcon continues to magnify and are kept updated to the new tech industry and is undoubtedly playing arole in shaping the future.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs by fostering innovation, investing, and positive change, providing mentorship, advocating for policies, and engaging with stakeholders.

Speakers

Aadeel Akhtar

Dr. Aadeel Akhtar is the CEO and Founder of PSYONIC, a company focused on developing advanced bionic limbs accessible to both humans and robots. He earned his Ph.D. in Neuroscience and M.S. in Electrical & Computer Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2016. Prior to that, he completed a B.S. in Biology in 2007 and an M.S. in Computer Science in 2008 at Loyola University Chicago. In recognition of his innovative work, Dr. Akhtar was named one of MIT Technology Review's top 35 Innovators Under 35 and one of America's Top 50 Disruptors by Newsweek in 2021.

Adam Alfi

Adam Alfi, a Partner at ICONIQ's growth-stage technology investing group, leads the Digital Leadership Board, a platform of influential Fortune500 business and technology leaders, and leads ICONIQ's global advisory network of CIO/CTO/CDOs, who serve as advisors and board members.

Adam Harris

Adam Harris, co-founder and CEO of Cloudbeds, is a hospitality management platform that has generated billions of dollars in revenue for thousands of properties worldwide. With a workforce of 700+ people in 40+ countries, Cloudbeds has been recognized for culture, product, and innovation. Harris and co-founder Richard Castle have won awards for their work. Before Cloudbeds, he was an entrepreneur with Bark4Beer, a dog-collar company. He serves as an advisor to San Diego startup incubator, Connect, and founded the La Jolla Community Fireworks Foundation.

Erin Angel

Dr. Angel, a medical tech executive with a strong scientific background and business acumen, oversees Research & Scientific Affairs for GE HealthCare. She has held leadership roles in scientific and medical affairs, product leadership, and general management for a large Computed Tomography business.

Lena Skliarova-Mordvinova

Lena, a serial entrepreneur, is the CTO and Co-Founder of Good Face Project, a chemistry-based AI SAAS platform for the Personal Care industry, and a published author of Image Recognition Algorithms.
